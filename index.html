My pet is a cat

Domestic cats are often called 'house cats' when kept as indoor pets. Cats have been domesticated (tamed) for nearly 10,000 years. They are one of the most popular pets in the world.

My Cat is white in color

Here is what my cat loves

  • Bone soup
  • Fish bones
  • Chasing rats
  • Playing with cushions

Cats are especially expressive creatures, and they're constantly telling you exactly how they're feeling – all you have to do is learn to “speak cat”! ... Cats have scent glands around their mouth, chin, and cheeks that leave behind their unique scent when rubbed. This behavior is known as “bunting.”

Cat communication, is the transfer of information by one or more cats that has an effect on the current or future behaviour of another animal, including humans. Cats use a range of communication modalities including vocal, visual, tactile and olfactory.
